Woman Lives for over 1000 Years!

A women has revealed to be hundreds of years old but is she to be believed? No animal of her kind has ever survived this long. When questions she seemed absolutely sure of herself, quote:

"I could never tell a lie."

Her family and friends have confirmed that she is telling the truth. The women would not reveal any kind of birth certificate saying that those didn't exist 1000 years ago.
